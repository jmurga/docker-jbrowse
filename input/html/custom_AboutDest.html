Dest is a population genomics-oriented genome browser, based on JBrowse, that contains DEST contains an unified dataset of pooled allele frequency estimates of D. melanogaster sampled across large portions of Europe and North America. This dataset is the result of the collaborative efforts of the DrosEU [cite Kapun et al. 2020] and DrosRTEC [cite - Machado et al.] consortia and combines both novel and previously published population genomic data. The browser visulize dataset which combines samples from 100 localities (e.g., orchards), 55 of which were sampled at two or more time points across the growing season (~10-15 generations) for one or more years. Collectively, these samples represent >13,000 individuals, cumulatively sequenced to >16,000X coverage.
 
DEST has been developed by the DrosEU and DrosRTEC consortia.
